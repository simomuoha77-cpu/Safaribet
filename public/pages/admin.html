⚖️ Betting Limits

📊 Platform Margin

Juan AI's odds already include their own bookmaker margin (typically ~5-8%). This is a SEPARATE, additional discount you control directly — shaves this extra percentage off the winnings portion of every real-market price (1X2, Double Chance, Over/Under, Both Teams to Score) before it's shown or bet against. Higher = safer for the platform, lower payouts for users. 0% = trust Juan AI's price as-is.
Recommended: 0-5%. Anything above 10% will look obviously worse than competitors and may push users away.
