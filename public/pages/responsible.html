⚠️ Warning Signs of Problem Gambling
  • 🔴Spending more than you can afford to lose
  • 🔴Chasing losses by placing bigger bets
  • 🔴Borrowing money to gamble
  • 🔴Hiding your gambling from family and friends
  • 🔴Gambling affecting your work, school or relationships
  • 🔴Feeling anxious, depressed or irritable about gambling
  • 🔴Unable to stop even when you want to
✅ Healthy Gambling Habits
  • 🟢Set a budget before you start and stick to it
  • 🟢Only bet what you can afford to lose
  • 🟢Treat gambling as entertainment, not income
  • 🟢Take regular breaks — don't gamble for hours straight
  • 🟢Balance gambling with other hobbies and activities
  • 🟢Never gamble when stressed, drunk or depressed
